Design Research

Design research focuses on the user ethnography, environment, psychology and anthropology. It plays an important role in making a product or service user friendly in all aspects. Intangible values like user aspirations and emotions that drive purchase decisions are studied in this phase. This process includes Primary Research (In-person user interaction to understand the target audience), Secondary Research (Obtaining information and insights from existing data- internet, statistical data, articles and books), Evaluative Research (Studying the outcome of products you have designed by making people use it), Exploratory Research (An open-ended study about a topic to gain unique meaningful insights) and Generative Research (Converting insights and research conclusions into design solutions).
