Thermocutter


Designing . Prototyping . Engineering









A thermocutter caters to a niche market in the northern European countries where Styrofoam is extensively used in floor construction as a heat insulator. This cutter is used to cut channels and joints in the Styrofoam for under-floor wires and pipelines. Advandes has developed a thermocutter for the European market with an objective to improve user safety and operating comfort. The product uses Stainless Steel (SS 304) blades as the heating element which provides for clean and dust free cutting.








This equipment is designed and engineered around the European anthropometric data to best suit the user profile. To provide maximum comfort, the thermocutter Grab Handle has a smooth and gentle form. The trigger that heats the blades has a safety lock to prevent accidents and ensure user safety. The extended stem of the cutter enables the user to stand up while cutting, allowing them to keep a better track and control over the path for cutting long stretches of styrofoam.









The adjustable Blade Holder accommodates blades of different profiles and sizes. The blade changing process is engineered to minimize the time and effort involved. The allen key needed to tighten/ loosen the screws is snapped on the thermocutter stem to avoid misplacement. The thermocutter casing is designed for easy servicing and maintenance of internal parts. The minimized footprint of the thermocutter base makes it agile and easily maneuverable, increasing productivity.

 

Advandes also designed a user guide and assembly guide for the thermocutter which includes the part nomenclature, part list, start-up and operation instructions and safety instructions. It's a compact and easy-to-make pamphlet with a simple cross-fold that can be carried along in your pocket
